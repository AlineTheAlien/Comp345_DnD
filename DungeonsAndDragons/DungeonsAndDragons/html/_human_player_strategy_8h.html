Detailed Description

Header file for the HumanPlayerStrategy.

This strategy is used by a player only. It allows the player to choose a target, to either attack the target, move (up to 6 grids since a fighter human's speed is of 30ft, with 5ft per grid as per the rules). The player may also perform other free actions, in a bigger D&D game, this would include using items other than equipment (such as a potion), but for the sake of simplicity, our game allows equipment modification during combat. This can be performed as many time as the player wants. A player may have multiple attacks as per the game rules.
